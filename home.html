How We Started

When I was in high school there was this really cute girl I really liked. She wasn't really into me so I had to impress her but I had nothing to impress her with. We planned a date and I picked her up in my rusted 2000 Toyota Land Cruiser. When she saw my car, she threatened to go home which made me so sad. But, she stuck around and we went on a walk around the park because I couldn't afford to take her out to eat. After the date, I drove to my apartment to hang out with her and she almost threw up because of the stench coming from it. It was a terrible house because I just moved out of my parents' home and didn't have much money. At the end of the night, she stopped talking to me and blocked me on all socials. This was all because of my terrible car and house and I hated the feeling. I realized that many men go through the exact same thing as me and I didn't want them to experience it either. A couple of buddies and myself did some brainstorming and thought of the idea to rent cars and houses. These wouldn't be lame but be the most expensive and nicest in order to impress your partner. If I tried to use a service like this when I was in high school, there would be absolutely no way to afford it which is why I made the cheapest prices. Some examples include Lamborghinis for $80 for the day or beachside houses for $50 a day. I bought some cars and houses initially and let my friends use them for free to test the waters and it worked magic. They are all happily married with the partners they went on the dates with because of how impressed they were. I personally am not married because I could not imagine only sticking with one spouse when I can get any partner I want very easily with this service. I have taken over 1,500 partners out in the last 6 months and that's not including the work vacation I took for a month to Dubai. Although we are currently only in North America, we have already made plans to expand to Europe, Africa, Asia and Antarctica. This is not for profit but to ensure nobody suffers what I suffered on my first date in high school.
